Information for Studying

Why do you need study tips?

Study skills are important for college students because they help you understand better, retain information, develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, manage time effectively and achieve better grades and test scores.

Techniques

  1. Find a good place to study
  2. Minimise distractions
  3. Take breaks
  4. Space out your studying
  5. Set study goals for each session
  6. Reward youself
  7. Study with a ground
  8. Take practice tests
  9. Use your own words
  10. Ask for help
  11. Take care of yourself
