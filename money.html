How to Create a Budget

  1. Identify Goals
      2. Set 3-5 financial goals like saving for a down payment or paying off your debt
  2. Record Expenses
      3. Estimate all your monthly expenses, including essentials and occasional cost
  3. Record Earnings
      4. Estimate your net income for the month. List each expected source of money you may receive.
  4. Calculate
      5. Subtract your expenses from your income. This gives you your budget balances
  5. Fix Week Spots
      6. If expenses exceed income adjust your spending or boost your income to stay balanced.
