Cover Letter

How to Write a Cover Letter

  1. Date and Contact Information
  2. Salutation/Greeting
  3. Opening Paragraph
  4. Middle Paragraph(s)
  5. Closing Paragraph
  6. Complimentary Close and Signature

Tips for Writing a Cover Letter

  1. Customize your header based on your application format
  2. Avoid generic references to your abilities
  3. Keep it short and to the point
  4. Proofread before you submit

Resume

How to Write a Resume

  1. Name and Contact Information
  2. Summary or Objective
  3. Professional Histroy (most recent first)
  4. Education
  5. Skills
  6. Optional

Tips for Writing a Resume

  1. Look for keywords in the job posting
  2. Review resume examples for your industry
  3. Use a professional font
  4. Include only relevant information
  5. Use active language
  6. Call attention to important achievements
  7. Only include subheading and sections you need
  8. Choose appropriate margins
  9. Proofread
  10. Make multiple versions of your resume
